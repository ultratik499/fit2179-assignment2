The Pulse of Melbourne

Analyzing Post-Pandemic Public Transport Recovery & CBD Foot Traffic

How has the morning commute changed for everyday Melburnians? By combining annual train network patronage with local pedestrian sensor clusters, this visualization maps out exactly where transit activity spills over directly onto our city streets. Hover over the nodes to see specific station entries and nearby street traffic statistics.

Author: Sam | Date: May 2026

Data Sources: Department of Transport / DataVic (Train Station Patronage) & City of Melbourne Open Data Portal (Pedestrian Counting System).
